FLOWER-Sometimes known as a Bloom or a Blossom


A flower is a part of a plant. Flowers are also called bloom or blossom of a plant. The flower gorws on a stock-thin node-which supports it. Flowers have petals. Inside the part of the flower that has petals are the parts which produce pollen and seeds.
